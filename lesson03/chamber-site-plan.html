Simba Chamber Of Commerce Site-Plan

Simba Chamber Of Commerce

The name "Simba Chamber of Commerce" evokes a sense of strength, unity, and community within the business world. "Simba" is a Swahili word that translates to "lion" in English, and lions are often seen as symbols of power, leadership, and courage.
In essence, "Simba Chamber of Commerce" is a name that signifies a robust, leadership-driven, and close community of businesses working together for their collective prosperity and success. It's a name that commands attention and conveys a sense of determination and unity among its members.

Site Purpose

The Simba Chamber Of Commerce offers a wealth of resources, including business guides, articles, templates, and links to government resources, to support local businesses in their growth and development. It facilitates networking opportunities among members through directories and event listings, encouraging collaboration and business partnerships. It serves as a valuable resource and communication platform that supports the growth and success of local businesses while strengthening the chamber's role as an advocate, connector, and facilitator within the community.

Scenarios

1. What networking events or workshops are scheduled for this month?

2. What community events or initiatives is the chamber currently involved in?

3. Is there a directory of local businesses on the chamber's website?

Color Schema

Dark Blue (#000080)

Heading Text: Use dark blue for the headings and subheadings throughout the website. This color choice provides a professional and authoritative appearance to important content.

Navigation Menu: Apply dark blue to the text of menu items in the website's navigation bar. This helps users easily identify and access different sections of the website.

Call-to-Action Buttons: Apply dark blue to the background or border of call-to-action buttons to make them stand out and encourage user interaction.

Dark Gray (#333333)

Paragraph Text: Dark gray is an excellent choice for the main body text and paragraphs. It provides good readability and contrast against a white background.

Footer Background: Apply dark gray as the background color for the website's footer section. This can create a visual separation between the main content and footer.

Borders and Dividers: Employ dark gray for borders and dividers between different sections or content blocks to provide structure and organization.
